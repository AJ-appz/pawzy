Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I use the app to identify a dog breed?
    A: Simply open the app, take a clear photo of the dog you want to identify, and our AI will analyze it and provide the most likely breed matches.
  • Q: Are there in-app purchases?
    A: Yes, Pawzy offers in-app purchases to unlock premium features like the Dog Poop Analyzer, AI Breed Mixer, and more.
  • Q: How accurate is the dog breed identification?
    A: Our AI is highly accurate, but results may vary depending on the quality of the photo and the uniqueness of the breed. We're constantly improving our algorithms for better accuracy.
  • Q: Does the app store my photos?
    A: No, Pawzy does not store any of your photos. Images are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after identification.
  • Q: How do the food and health analysis features work?
    A: These features use AI to analyze the nutritional content of dog food and provide health insights. However, they are for informational purposes only and should not replace professional veterinary advice.
